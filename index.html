The Big Finned Squid(Magnapinna)

What is a big finned squid?

The Big finned Squid a super long squid that lives so deep in the ocean they are rarely seen. In fact they live deeper in the ocean than any other known squid.     

A Creepy but cool picture of the squid

As usual as it is to see, it is known to eat like other squids by using two tentacles to catch its prey and all its arms to hold it while it eats.

But just How Big can A Big Finned Squid Get?

In Well Lit Conditions

Big Finned squids are rarely seen and as of 2021 there was roughly only a dozen sighting around the entire world! No adults have been seen in person, so their actual length is unknown. However they have been estimated to reach 26 feet long!
